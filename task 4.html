A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

Tribute page



This is a tribute page to A.P.J.Abdul Kalam. Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs. He was president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was known as the 'Missile Man of India' for his work in the development of ballistic missile technology. From 1992 to 1999, Kalam was the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and the Secretary of the DRDO. Kalam was the Chief Project Coordinator during the Pokhran II tests.:

  • 1.He joined the Defence Research and Development Organization (DRDO) as a scientist after graduation
  • 2.He joined the Indian Space research Organization (ISRO) in 1969.
  • 3.Kalam Sir served as the Project Director of Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle III (PSLV III).
  • 4.His dexterity in space science made him popular as the 'Missile Man of India'.

A.P.J.Abdul Kalam is a true hero of the came to be known as the Missile Man of India for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. He also played a pivotal organisational, technical, and political role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998, the first since the original nuclear test by India in 1974.
